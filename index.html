Our Legacy

Established on July 1, 1921, B.K. Mariappa's Hostel has provided free lodging to thousands of deserving students for over a century.

The Ashoka Sangha (Est. 1947) continues to foster excellence through annual inter-hostel competitions, keeping the spirit of community alive.
